ABOUT US

Founded by a journalist with carpentry roots, Shree Hans has proudly upheld its family heritage by crafting premium handicrafts and furniture for over a decade. Based in Jodhpur, our journey began as a trusted supplier to leading exporters, evolving with a successful relaunch two years ago. Establishing our own factory, we merged ancestral craftsmanship with modern innovation, elevating our craft to new heights. With a production facility spanning 4,000 square meters and state-of-the-art machinery, we specialize in the finest combinations of wood, metal, and stone. Our skilled workforce delivers four to five containers monthly, and our infrastructure supports further expansion as we grow in the global market. At Shree Hans, we are committed to preserving traditional craftsmanship while advancing through modern design and innovation. We envision ourselves as global leaders in handcrafted furniture and decor, known for quality and artistry. Our mission is to craft bespoke furniture and handicrafts that reflect generational passion and skill. We strive to be a reliable partner for clients locally and internationally, delivering excellence in every project. Our portfolio includes diverse projects across major Indian cities such as Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai, Jaisalmer, and Jodhpur, along with ongoing collaborations in Europe. Each piece reflects a balance of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design, offering custom solutions for unique client needs. As we continue to grow, we aim to enter the international export market, build long-term partnerships, and leverage our production capabilities to deliver unparalleled craftsmanship globally. Our goal is to set new standards in design and production, becoming synonymous with luxury and artistic excellence.
